# Privacy Policy

**Invoker** does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

- All saved requests are stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local` and never leave your machine.
- HTTP requests are made directly from your browser to the URLs you specify. No data passes through any third-party server.
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

For questions, open an issue at https://github.com/ran1979/invoker-extension
